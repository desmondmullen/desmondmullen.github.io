SpaceElephant privacy policy

This policy effective December 14, 2023

SpaceElephant is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect any personal information of any sort, nor do we use cookies or any other tracking methods. Your use of the Services indicates you agree to this policy and our terms of use.

Summary

Nothing in this policy contradicts the following statements:

  1. We do not collect any of your personal information, including your IP address.
  2. We do not sell your personal information to advertisers or other third parties.

Complete terms

As used in this policy, “Personal Information” is data that can identify a particular person or device. Aggregate data is not considered Personal Information.

1. SpaceElephant never collects your Personal Information

Our Services do not collect any of your Personal Information. Unlike many apps, our app does not collect your IP address. We do detect non-personally identifiable geo-location information to optimize our services, but we definitely do not collect your precise geo-location or associate geo-location information with a particular user.

2. SpaceElephant never sells any of your Personal Information

We do not collect any of your Personal Information. We never sell any of your Personal Information to third parties. Further, we do not allow third parties to collect info about you in our app through cookies or other means.

Changes to this policy

We may make small, inconsequential changes to this policy with or without notice to you, so you are encouraged to review the policy from time to time.
