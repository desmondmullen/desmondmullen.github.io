Legal Aid Intake Demo – Optional SMS Opt-In

Prototype operated by Desmond Mullen. This is a proof-of-concept demonstrating how a nonprofit legal aid organization might use SMS for eligibility screening and intake. It is not affiliated with any nonprofit.

Program Overview

  • Program name: Legal Aid Intake Demo (Informational, user-initiated)
  • Sender: Desmond Mullen – Legal Aid Intake Demo
  • Purpose: Send eligibility screening prompts and intake questions via SMS
  • Message frequency: Varies based on user interaction
  • No marketing: Messages are informational and user-initiated

Important: SMS is optional and is not required to use or view this demo.

SMS Consent Terms

By opting in, you agree to receive SMS messages from Desmond Mullen – Legal Aid Intake Demo for eligibility screening and intake questions. Message frequency varies. Reply STOP to unsubscribe, HELP for help. Message & data rates may apply. You confirm you are the owner or customary user of the mobile number provided. Consent is not a condition of any service, benefit, or employment opportunity.

Use the Demo Without SMS

You can continue without receiving text messages.

Optional: Receive SMS Messages

If you’d like to receive SMS prompts, provide your mobile number and give consent below.

Enter in international format if possible (e.g., +19195551234). US numbers without “+1” are accepted.

Submitting without checking the consent box will not enroll you in SMS. You may still use the demo.

Alternative: Keyword Opt-In

You can also opt in by texting a keyword from your mobile device:

Text to opt in: START or YES to (855) 303-1412
Opt-out anytime: STOP CANCEL UNSUBSCRIBE
Help: HELP INFO

Keyword opt-in is an alternative path; it is not required to view or use the demo.

What to Expect

After you opt in, you’ll receive a welcome text and then interactive prompts. Example:

Welcome to the Legal Aid Intake Demo by Desmond Mullen. This demo guides you through eligibility and intake questions. Reply START to begin. Reply STOP to unsubscribe, HELP for help. Msg&data rates may apply.
