About

My digital path in life has gone from learning BASIC in college (where we wrote our assignments on paper because "personal computers" were still in their infancy) to being Digital Technical Director on a music video for Paul McCartney to Silicon Valley to digital show production at a planetarium and many places in-between, but has always had components of cutting-edge tech, Internet, and building useful tools.

Here are a few stops along that path:

  • developed graphical Battleship-style game on Dad's Apple IIe in the early '80s
  • created icons for the historic Bloomberg Terminal in the late '80s
  • Digital Technical Director for Paul McCartney's "Off the Ground" music video at Industrial Light and Magic in '93
  • created and released communications app called "Messenger" in '94 with all the features that AOL and others would later make famous in their same-named products
  • early team member in two pioneering Silicon Valley startups in the mid-to-late-90s
  • introduced and implemented full digital show production at a world-famous planetarium in the early 2000s
  • created award-winning 3D-animated children's DVD (now on Amazon streaming video) in '05
  • developed key heuristic solutions for workflow management and load-balancing of customer work at a successful motorcycle shop in the later 2010s
  • currently formalizing and updating my skills in a top-5 university coding bootcamp

Technical Competencies: Expert skills with Javascript, jQuery, Firebase, HTML, CSS, Photoshop, Adobe Creative Suite, Excel, VBA, FileMaker Pro, Applescript, Final Cut Pro, ProTools, MS Office. High level of competence with SQL, git, systems administration, networks, servers, desktops, laptops and various mobile and peripheral devices.

Core Competencies: Excellent communication, organizational and problem-solving skills. Excellent workflow management skills, especially in establishing priorities and accomplishing multiple tasks in a timely manner for effective project management.
