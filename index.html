About

My digital path in life has gone from learning to code on Dad's Apple II Plus, to being Digital Technical Director on a music video for Paul McCartney, to coding with JavaScript precursors in two Silicon Valley startups, to digital show production at a world-famous planetarium, to UNC's excellent bootcamp to polish my skills, and finally to skilled MERN developer.

My detail-oriented, comprehensive approach has consistently yielded significant improvements in products and processes, and strong realizations of initial designs and concepts. What has driven me consistently and connected all these points in my career are the components of creative problem-solving, cutting-edge tech, coding, logic, and delivering innovative solutions. For me, MERN development is the bringing-together of all these disciplines into one.

Here are a few stops along that path:

  • developed graphical Battleship-style game on Dad's Apple IIe in the early '80s
  • created icons for the historic Bloomberg Terminal in the late '80s
  • Digital Technical Director for Paul McCartney's "Off the Ground" music video at Industrial Light and Magic in '93
  • created and released communications app called "Messenger" in '94 with all the features that AOL and others would later make famous in their same-named products
  • early team member in two pioneering Silicon Valley startups in the mid-to-late-90s
  • introduced and implemented full digital show production at a world-famous planetarium in the early 2000s
  • created award-winning 3D-animated children's DVD (now on Amazon streaming video) in '05
  • developed key heuristic solutions for workflow management and load-balancing of customer work at a successful motorcycle shop in the later 2010s
  • currently formalizing and updating my skills in a top-5 university coding bootcamp

Technical Competencies: Expert skills in MERN Full Stack Development including Javascript, HTML, CSS, MongoDB, Express React, Node, Axios, APIs, SQL, jQuery, Visual Studio Code, Mobile Development, git, GitHub, Heroku, Firebase, Photoshop, Adobe Creative Suite, Excel, VBA, FileMaker Pro, Applescript, Final Cut Pro, ProTools, MS Office.

Core Competencies: Strong ability to quickly get up to speed with new technologies, languages, and paradigms. Excellent communication, organizational and problem-solving skills. Seasoned workflow management skills, especially in establishing priorities and accomplishing multiple tasks in a timely manner for effective project management.
